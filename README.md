# Blypan Player Tracker ⚽🤖

A lightweight, AI-powered web application designed for grassroots youth football coaches to track, analyse, and accelerate player development using the **FA’s Four Corners Framework**. 

Built as a capstone project for the **Google AI Professional** qualification, this application demonstrates rapid prototyping ("vibe coding"), data instantiation, and practical Generative AI integration to solve a real-world coaching challenge.

## 📋 The Problem & Product Vision

### The Problem
Grassroots football coaches often struggle to maintain consistent, structured development tracking for their players. Traditional methods (spreadsheets or paper notes) are fragmented, time-consuming, and fail to translate raw observations into actionable training plans. 

### The Vision
To create an intuitive, AI-assisted tool that allows a coach to quickly input qualitative comments and quantitative metrics, instantly generating structured, hyper-targeted training focuses tailored to individual player needs.

---

## 🧠 Core Features & Frameworks

### 1. The FA Four Corners Alignment
The app rates players from **1 (Needs Focus)** to **5 (Outstanding)** across the core pillars of UK youth development:
* **Technical/Tactical:** Ball mastery, game intelligence, positional awareness.
* **Physical:** Speed, stamina, agility, core strength.
* **Psychological:** Decision-making, confidence, resilience.
* **Social:** Communication, teamwork, attitude, and leadership.

### 2. AI-Powered Evaluation Engine
By combining the quantitative 1-5 metrics with the coach's raw text comments, the integrated LLM acts as an expert coaching consultant. It processes the dataset to output:
* A holistic evaluation of the player's current developmental stage.
* 2-3 highly specific training focuses for the upcoming weeks.

### 3. Longitudinal Progression Tracking
The application supports multiple evaluation instances over time, allowing coaches to visually track a player's development curve and see if targeted training interventions are yielding results over the course of the season.

## 📈 Product Management Takeaways

Building this app provided direct, hands-on experience with:
* **Scoping MVP:** Translating a personal workflow bottleneck into a minimalist, high-value feature set.
* **AI Product Design:** Learning how to bound LLM outputs to prevent hallucinations and ensure advice remains aligned with recognized athletic frameworks (FA Four Corners).
* **Prompt Engineering:** Structuring system prompts to ensure the AI speaks in an encouraging, constructive, and professionally astute coaching tone.
